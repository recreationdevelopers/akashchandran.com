About Akash

Akash is a singer songwriter currently working as a full time music composer at BYJU'S. He is a KM Music Conservatory (by A.R Rahman) pass out and has experience in programming, arranging, mixing and mastering. He also works as the lead vocalist in the Kerala based band Thamarassery Churam. 

He has been working on his singles and collaborating with other artists as Composer, Singer, Programmer and Vocal Arranger. He is also efficient at instruments like Guitar, Keyboard and specialises in manual vocal pitch correction and live finger drumming.
